Health Monitix

This application is designed to transform the landscape of patient health monitoring by providing a user-friendly and cost-effective solution that harnesses smartphone technology. It allows users to effortlessly measure their heart rate and blood pressure using, as well as track their daily physical activity, thereby removing the need for expensive sensors or specialized equipment. The app features a dual-access system, enabling seamless interaction between doctors and patients. Healthcare providers can access comprehensive dashboards to monitor patient metrics and offer personalized feedback, while patients can view their health data and track their progress over time. With an emphasis on simplicity and accessibility, the application empowers patients to take control of their health, encouraging proactive management and fostering better communication with healthcare professionals. By streamlining data collection and enhancing engagement, this innovative solution aims to improve health outcomes and create a collaborative approach to healthcare in an increasingly digital world.

In summary, The important objective for health monitix are:
  • User-Fiendly Monitoring: Patients can easily measure heart rate and blood pressure simplifying health tracking.
  • Cost-Effective Solution: Eliminates the need for expensive sensors, making health monitoring accessible to more individuals.
  • Dual Access for Users: Both doctors and patients have separate logins to view and manage health metrics effectively.
  • Real-Time Data Tracking: Enables patients to monitor daily physical activity alongside vital signs for a comprehensive health overview.
  • Enhanced Communication: Improves interaction between patients and healthcare providers, allowing for timely feedback and personalized care

Tech Stack:

  • MySQL – Database
  • PHP – Backend
  • HTML – Page Layout and Design
  • CSS – Design
  • JavaScript – Frontend
